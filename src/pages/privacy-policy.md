# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: April 3, 2026*

Welcome to **Encelerate** (encelerate.com). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website, specifically our comment section.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to comment on our blog using Google OAuth, we collect basic profile information provided by Google, including:
- **Your Name**: Used to display your identity on comments.
- **Your Email Address**: Used for account identification and optional nested-reply notifications.
- **Your Profile Picture**: Used to display an avatar next to your comments.

We do not collect any other personal data unless you explicitly provide it to us.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:
- **Identity**: To identify you as the author of your comments.
- **Notifications**: To send you email alerts when someone replies to your comment (if you have opted-in to notifications).
- **Security & Integrity**: To prevent spam and maintain the quality of the discussion on our platform.

## 3. Data Retention & Third Parties

Your data is stored and processed using the following infrastructure:
- **Cloudflare Workers & D1**: For hosting our application and storing comment data.
- **AWS SES (Amazon Simple Email Service)**: Used exclusively to deliver notification emails.
- **Google OAuth**: Used for secure authentication.

We **do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer** your personally identifiable information to outside parties for marketing or any other unauthorized purpose.

## 4. Data Control

You have full control over your information. At any point, you can request a copy of your data or ask us to permanently delete your account and comments by emailing **welcome@encelerate.com**.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data control, please contact us at **welcome@encelerate.com**.
